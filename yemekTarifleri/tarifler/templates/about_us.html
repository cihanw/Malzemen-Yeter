We are a team of four passionate individuals dedicated to creating an interactive recipe website that brings people together through food. Our project aims to provide a user-friendly platform for sharing, discovering, and exploring a wide variety of recipes. Each of us brings unique skills and perspectives to the project: from web development to database management, and backend design to user experience. Together, we have worked to create a seamless and enjoyable online experience that allows users to find and share their favorite dishes with ease. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, our goal is to inspire creativity and make cooking accessible for everyone. Join us on our journey as we combine technology and culinary arts to make cooking more fun and engaging! Y.S.Ş.C.
